Tea

Description

A tea is an integral part of different asian cusine. It is enjoyed best in the winter season.

Ingredients

  • Spices powder
  • Milk
  • Tea leaves

Steps to make the perfect tea!

  1. Put some water on a kettle to boil
  2. Add tea leaves and your desired flavors in the boiling water
  3. Boil for a solid minutes
  4. Serve and enjoy!
